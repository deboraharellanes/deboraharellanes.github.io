Getting Started

A VW engine has a small, economical, 4 stroke design. It should be tuned at least every 5000 to 10,000 miles to ensure efficient running. If the engine is out of tune, then it will not run well, have poor gas mileage, and will wear out sooner. Furthermore, if the car is driven extremely hard, or under extreme weather conditions, or it is a modified high performance engine, then it should be tuned every 2000 to 3000 miles. A tune up and maintenance check can take up to two hours. Be sure to put 2x4 woods blocks in front of the wheels so it can't roll when it is out of gear. Also the solvents and chemicals are toxic. Please use latex nitrile gloves and if you are working indoors, a breathing mask is a good option. A car has up to 800 moving parts. The following listing the basics to keep a car running well. There are many more components that need to be maintained and could even be replaced if you want to restore the car.
