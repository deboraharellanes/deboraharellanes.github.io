Fan Housing Removal

  1. Remove the two heater hoses and the generator strap
  2. Pull the lead wire out of the ignition coil
  3. Remove the distributor cap and the spark plug connectors
  4. Remove the retaining screws on both sides of the fan housing
  5. Remove the outer half of the generator pulley and remove the fan belt
  6. Remove the thermostat securing screw and take out the thermastat
  7. Remove the bottom of the carburetor preheater duct
  8. Remove the fan housing
  9. Make sure fan housing is clear of debris
  10. Make sure air deflector plates are securely clipped on

Cooling Fan Removal

  1. Remove the four retaining screws with a T-wrench
  2. Remove the generator and fan
  3. Unscrew the retaining nut and take off the fan, spacers washers, and the hub
  4. Now that the cooling fan has been removed the oil cooler is accessible
