Equipment Needed to do an Oil Change

  • 3 quarts of 30W oil
  • Oil strainer
  • Sump plate
  • Gasket kit (2 paper gaskets & 7 copper washers)
  • Oil pan drain
  • Screw driver
  • 21 millometer or adjustable wrench
  • Funnel

Oil Change Steps

  1. Run the car for about 15 minutes to warm up the oil
  2. Jack up the back of the car enough to comfortably roll under it on a creeper
  3. Jack up the front of the car to level it out
  4. Place the oil pan directly under the sump plate and then loosen the six nuts
  5. Oil will begin draining into the oil pan
  6. Remove the sump plate and oil strainer along with the gaskets
  7. Let the oil drain for about fifteen minutes until it is barely dripping
  8. Scrape off any gasket pieces stuck on the engine block
  9. Clean the sump plate and oil strainer with solvent
  10. Clean up the engine area
  11. Install new gaskets and sump plate
  12. Carefully tighten the nuts
  13. Remove the jack stands and let the car down
  14. Refill the engine with oil
